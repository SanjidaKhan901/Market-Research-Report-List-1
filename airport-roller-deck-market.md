<p><ul><li>Belt Connected</li><li>Chain Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airport Roller Deck market consists of two primary types: Belt Connected and Chain Connected systems. Belt Connected roller decks utilize flexible belts to transport luggage, providing smooth and quiet operation ideal for efficient baggage handling in terminals. In contrast, Chain Connected roller decks rely on sturdy chains for movement, offering higher durability and load capacity, making them suitable for heavier cargo areas. Each type serves unique operational requirements, enhancing the overall efficiency of airport logistics and baggage management.</p></p>
