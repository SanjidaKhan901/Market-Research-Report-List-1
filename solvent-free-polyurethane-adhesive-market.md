<p><p>The solvent-free polyurethane adhesive market comprises two main types: monocomponent and bi-component adhesives. Monocomponent adhesives are ready-to-use, offering convenience and ease of application, as they cure upon exposure to moisture in the air. In contrast, bi-component adhesives consist of two separate components that must be mixed before application, providing enhanced performance and flexibility in various bonding applications. Both types are favored for their strong adhesion properties and reduced environmental impact due to the absence of solvents.</p></p>
