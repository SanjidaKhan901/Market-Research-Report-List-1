<p><p>The organoclay powder market is categorized into three types based on polarity. Low Polarity (Type I) organoclay powders are suitable for non-polar systems, often utilized in applications like sealants and adhesives. Medium Polarity (Type II) powders balance attraction to both polar and non-polar substances, making them versatile for coatings and plastics. High Polarity (Type III) organoclay powders interact with polar solvents, commonly found in paints, lubricants, and personal care products, enhancing thickness and stability in formulations.</p></p>
