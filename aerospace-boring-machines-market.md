The market is segmented into two primary types based on spindle orientation: Vertical Spindle Orientation and Horizontal Spindle Orientation. Vertical spindle machines are designed for operations requiring vertical access, enabling precision in machining operations like engine components. In contrast, horizontal spindle machines provide enhanced stability and support for larger workpieces, facilitating efficient drilling and machining processes in complex aerospace structures.</p></p>
