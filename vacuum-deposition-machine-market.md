<p><p>The vacuum deposition machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). PVD involves physical processes such as evaporation or sputtering to deposit thin films on surfaces, commonly used for decorative coatings and semiconductor applications. CVD, on the other hand, relies on chemical reactions to produce solid materials deposited onto substrates, making it ideal for advanced materials like coatings in aerospace and electronics. Both methods are vital for various industrial applications.</p></p>
