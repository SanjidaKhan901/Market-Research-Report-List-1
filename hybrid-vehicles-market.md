<p><p>Hybrid vehicles are categorized into three main types based on their powertrain configurations. **串联式混合动力 (Series Hybrid)** uses an engine to generate electricity for an electric motor that drives the wheels, eliminating direct mechanical connection. **并联式混合动力 (Parallel Hybrid)** allows both the engine and electric motor to drive the wheels simultaneously or independently, offering flexibility. **混联式混合动力 (Series-Parallel Hybrid)** combines both systems, enabling the vehicle to switch between series and parallel modes for optimal efficiency and performance in varying driving conditions.</p></p>
