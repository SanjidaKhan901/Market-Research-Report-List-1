<p><p>A Content Delivery Network (CDN) is a distributed network of servers strategically located across various geographical locations, designed to deliver web content, applications, and media to users efficiently and reliably. By caching content closer to end-users, CDNs reduce latency, improve load times, and enhance the overall user experience. They are essential for organizations seeking to manage increased web traffic, particularly during peak times, while ensuring content availability and security.</p><p>The CDN market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rise in digital media consumption, advancements in streaming technology, and the increasing importance of website performance. Mobile device proliferation and the acceleration of e-commerce have also fueled demand for CDN services, as businesses strive to deliver seamless experiences across platforms. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period, reflecting a robust shift toward cloud-based solutions and the growing emphasis on cybersecurity features within CDN offerings.</p><p>Recent trends include the adoption of edge computing, which minimizes latency by processing data nearer to the source, and the proliferation of video content, necessitating advanced streaming capabilities. As businesses continue to digitalize, CDNs will play a critical role in shaping online interaction and content consumption.</p></p>
