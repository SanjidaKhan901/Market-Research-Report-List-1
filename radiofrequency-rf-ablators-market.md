<p><strong>The Radiofrequency (RF) Ablators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temperature-Controlled Rf Ablators</li><li>Fluid-Cooled Rf Ablators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiofrequency (RF) ablation market includes two primary types: temperature-controlled RF abulators and fluid-cooled RF abulators. Temperature-controlled RF abulators maintain a consistent thermal environment during procedures, ensuring precision and reducing the risk of damage to surrounding tissues. Fluid-cooled RF abulators utilize circulating fluid to dissipate heat, allowing for the delivery of higher energy levels while minimizing thermal spread. Both types enhance the efficacy of ablation procedures, targeting tissue effectively for various medical applications, including cardiac and tumor ablation therapies.</p></p>
