<p><strong>The Capnography Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mainstream Capnographys</li><li>Sidestream Capnographys</li><li>Microstream Capnographys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capnography equipment market is categorized into three main types: mainstream, sidestream, and microstream capnographs. Mainstream capnographs measure exhaled carbon dioxide directly in-line with the breathing circuit, offering real-time readings. Sidestream capnographs collect gas samples through a tube, allowing flexibility in monitoring but with some delay in readings. Microstream capnographs utilize a low-flow sample technique for continuous monitoring, ideal for low-resource settings. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, enhancing respiratory monitoring in various healthcare environments.</p></p>
