<p><p>The Haptic Feedback Surgical System market comprises two primary types: Kinesthetic Feedback Systems and Cutaneous Feedback Systems. Kinesthetic Feedback Systems provide users with force and motion sensations, emulating the resistance and weight of surgical instruments, enhancing the realism of procedures. Cutaneous Feedback Systems, on the other hand, deliver tactile sensations through vibrations or pressure, simulating contact with tissues. Together, these systems enhance the surgeon's perception and control during operations, contributing to improved precision and safety in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
