<p><p>The solar panel module market comprises three main types: monocrystalline silicon solar PV, polycrystalline silicon solar PV, and thin-film solar PV. Monocrystalline panels offer high efficiency and space-saving benefits, ideal for limited areas. Polycrystalline panels are cost-effective, utilising multiple silicon crystals, though with slightly lower efficiency. Thin-film solar PV features lightweight materials and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications, including building-integrated systems. Each type serves distinct market needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and application versatility.</p></p>
