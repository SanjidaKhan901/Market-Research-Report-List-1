<p><p>The Wing Anti-icing System market includes Mechanical De-icing Systems, which use inflatable boots to break ice off wings; Electric Pulse Anti-icing Systems, which generate shockwaves to dislodge ice; and Electric Heating Anti-icing Systems, which use heated elements to prevent ice formation. Mechanical systems are cost-effective but less efficient, while electric pulse systems are lightweight and energy-efficient. Electric heating systems offer precise ice prevention but consume more power. Each system caters to specific aircraft needs, balancing efficiency, weight, and operational costs.</p></p>
