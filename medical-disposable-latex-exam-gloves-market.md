<p><p>The medical disposable latex exam gloves market comprises two primary types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are treated with a dusting powder, usually cornstarch, which facilitates easier donning and removal but may cause sensitivity or allergic reactions in some users. Non-powdered gloves, on the other hand, are made without powder, reducing the risk of allergic reactions and contamination, making them suitable for sensitive environments. Both types are crucial for infection control and ensure safety in medical settings.</p></p>
