<p><p>The consumer drones market includes three main types: multi-rotor, fixed-wing, and single rotor. Multi-rotor drones, equipped with multiple propellers, offer stability and versatility, making them popular for aerial photography and recreational use. Fixed-wing drones resemble traditional aircraft, providing longer flight times and greater range, suitable for mapping and surveying tasks. Single rotor drones combine features of both multi-rotor and fixed-wing designs, typically delivering higher efficiency and endurance, making them ideal for specialized applications such as agriculture and inspections.</p></p>
