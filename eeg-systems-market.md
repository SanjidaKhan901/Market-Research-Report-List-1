<p><strong>The EEG Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional EEG</li><li>Video EEG</li><li>Dynamic EEG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EEG systems market comprises three main types: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG involves standard electrode placement to record electrical brain activity, primarily used for diagnosing seizures and sleep disorders. Video EEG combines EEG data with video monitoring to correlate brain activity with physical symptoms, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. Dynamic EEG captures brain activity during routine daily activities, providing insights into natural brain function and disorders in real-world settings, thus improving patient assessment and treatment planning.</p></p>
