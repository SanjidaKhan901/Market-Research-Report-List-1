<p><ul><li>Cryopreserved Amniotic Membranes</li><li>Dehydrated Amniotic Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amniotic products market includes two main types: cryopreserved amniotic membranes and dehydrated amniotic membranes. Cryopreserved amniotic membranes are stored at low temperatures to maintain their cellular integrity and growth factors, making them suitable for surgical applications and wound healing. In contrast, dehydrated amniotic membranes have the moisture removed, extending their shelf life and ease of use while retaining essential properties for promoting tissue regeneration. Both types are significant in regenerative medicine and supporting various therapeutic procedures.</p></p>
