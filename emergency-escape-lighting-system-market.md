<p><ul><li>Central Emergency Lighting</li><li>Self-contained Emergency Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Escape Lighting System Market comprises two main types: Central Emergency Lighting and Self-contained Emergency Lighting. Central Emergency Lighting systems are centralized solutions, where a single power source provides backup lighting to multiple fixtures throughout a facility, ensuring uniform illumination during emergencies. In contrast, Self-contained Emergency Lighting systems are independent units equipped with their own battery and light source, allowing them to operate autonomously when the main power fails, enhancing flexibility and easier installation in various settings.</p></p>
