<p><p>The Low Sugar Pectin Amidated market is segmented into High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin. HM Pectin requires higher sugar concentrations and is typically used in traditional jams and jellies, providing a gel-like texture. In contrast, LM Pectin functions effectively with lower sugar levels and is ideal for sugar-reduced products, enabling applications in various food items like sauces and dairy. Both types cater to distinct consumer preferences for texture and health-conscious choices in food products.</p></p>
