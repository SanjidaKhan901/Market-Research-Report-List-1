<p><p>The Auto Relay Market encompasses various types, including Plug-In Relays and PCB (Printed Circuit Board) Relays. Plug-In Relays are easily replaceable components that mount into a socket, allowing for simple connections and maintenance in automotive applications. PCB Relays, on the other hand, are soldered directly onto circuit boards, offering a compact design ideal for space-constrained environments. Both types serve crucial roles in automotive systems, controlling electrical flows and enhancing vehicle safety and efficiency.</p></p>
