<p><p>Advanced Drug Delivery Systems encompass various methods that enhance the efficacy and targeting of medications. Oral drug delivery is the most common, allowing for easy administration and patient compliance. Pulmonary drug delivery utilizes inhalation for direct lung access, ideal for respiratory treatments. Transdermal delivery involves patches that deliver drugs through the skin, ensuring steady absorption. Parenteral delivery includes injections, providing rapid drug action and bypassing gastrointestinal limitations. Together, these systems improve therapeutic outcomes and patient adherence across diverse medical conditions.</p></p>
