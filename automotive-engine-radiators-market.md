<p><ul><li>Aluminum Core Radiator</li><li>Copper Core Radiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine radiator market primarily consists of two types: aluminum core radiators and copper core radiators. Aluminum core radiators are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and offer efficient heat dissipation, making them popular in modern vehicles. In contrast, copper core radiators, known for their excellent thermal conductivity, provide superior performance but are heavier and more expensive to produce. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal engine temperatures, influencing vehicle performance and longevity, with market trends reflecting shifts in material preferences and manufacturing technologies.</p></p>
