<p><ul><li>Top-Entry Agitator</li><li>Side-Entry Agitator</li><li>Bottom-Entry Agitator</li><li>Portable Agitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial agitators market comprises several types based on their mounting and application. Top-entry agitators are designed for large tanks, effectively mixing from the top down. Side-entry agitators are utilized in horizontal tanks, ensuring efficient mixing without significantly disrupting the tank's contents. Bottom-entry agitators are ideal for deep vessels, delivering energy directly to the bottom for optimal blending. Portable agitators offer flexibility and mobility, allowing for effective mixing in various locations and applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
