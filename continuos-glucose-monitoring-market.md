<p><ul><li>Retrospective CGM (r-CGM)</li><li>Real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) market is primarily divided into two types: retrospective CGM (r-CGM) and real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM). r-CGM systems provide users with historical glucose data after the monitoring period, allowing for analysis of trends and patterns. In contrast, RT-CGM provides ongoing, real-time glucose readings, alerting users to fluctuations and enabling immediate responses to changes. Both types play crucial roles in diabetes management, enhancing patient awareness and control over their glucose levels.</p></p>
