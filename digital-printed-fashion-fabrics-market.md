<p><strong>The Digital Printed Fashion Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Printing</li><li>Disperse Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital printed fashion fabrics market encompasses various printing techniques, notably direct printing and disperse printing. Direct printing involves applying ink directly onto fabrics, allowing for high-detail designs and a wide color range, making it ideal for cotton and other absorbent materials. Disperse printing, on the other hand, uses sublimation inks that transfer dyes to synthetic fabrics, such as polyester, by heating. This method results in vibrant and long-lasting prints, making it popular in sportswear and fashion apparel.</p></p>
