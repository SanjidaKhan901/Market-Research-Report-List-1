<p><p>The aircraft fly-by-wire system market comprises two main types: Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems and Fully Fly-By-Wire Control Systems. Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems enhance safety by providing manual control as a backup to electronic systems, ensuring redundancy in critical situations. In contrast, Fully Fly-By-Wire Control Systems replace traditional mechanical controls with electronic interfaces that improve responsiveness, reduce weight, and enable advanced flight management capabilities. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing aircraft performance and safety.</p></p>
