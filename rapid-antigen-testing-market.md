<p><strong>The Rapid Antigen Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunosorbent Assay</li><li>Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent</li><li>Precipitation Reaction</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Agglutination Reaction</li><li>Complement Fixatio</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Antigen Testing market includes various types of assays designed for quick detection of antigens in samples. Immunosorbent assays utilize antibodies to capture target antigens. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) employ enzymes to produce detectable signals. Precipitation reactions involve the formation of insoluble complexes. Radioimmunoassays use radioactive isotopes for detection. Agglutination reactions rely on the clumping of particles, while complement fixation tests detect the presence of specific antigens through complement activation. Each method offers unique benefits for rapid diagnostics.</p></p>
