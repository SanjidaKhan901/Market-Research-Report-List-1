<p><p>The Aluminum Die Casting market is categorized into three main types: Low Pressure Die Casting, High Pressure Die Casting, and Other methods. Low Pressure Die Casting involves forcing molten aluminum into molds under low pressure, resulting in thinner walls and complex shapes. High Pressure Die Casting uses high pressure to inject aluminum quickly, offering excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy for high-volume production. The Other category includes techniques like gravity die casting and sand casting, catering to specialized applications in various industries.</p></p>
