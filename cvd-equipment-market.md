<p><strong>The CVD Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PECVD</li><li>MOCVD</li><li>ThermalCVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical vapor deposition (CVD) equipment market comprises several types, including Plasma-Enhanced CVD (PECVD), Metal-Organic CVD (MOCVD), and Thermal CVD. PECVD utilizes plasma to enhance chemical reactions at lower temperatures, making it ideal for delicate substrates like semiconductors. MOCVD employs metal-organic compounds to deposit thin films, primarily used in optoelectronics and LEDs. Thermal CVD relies on heat to promote chemical reactions, typically providing high-quality coatings for various applications. Each technique serves distinct needs in semiconductor and materials manufacturing.</p></p>
