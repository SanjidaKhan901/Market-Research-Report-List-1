<p><p>The metal processing lubricant market encompasses three primary types: quenching, cutting, and grinding lubricants, along with corrosion preventives. Quenching lubricants are used in heat treatment processes to cool and harden metal parts. Cutting lubricants facilitate smoother and more efficient machining operations by reducing friction and improving tool life. Grinding lubricants enhance the grinding process by cooling and lubricating the grinding wheel. Corrosion preventives protect metal surfaces from rust and degradation, ensuring durability and extending the lifespan of manufactured components.</p></p>
