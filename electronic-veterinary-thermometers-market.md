<p><p>The electronic veterinary thermometer market is primarily categorized into anal and ear types. Anal thermometers measure body temperature rectally, providing accurate readings for pets, especially for larger animals or those that are difficult to handle. Ear thermometers, on the other hand, use infrared technology to measure temperature from the ear canal, offering a quick and non-invasive alternative suitable for smaller animals. Both types cater to the needs of veterinarians and pet owners, prioritizing accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
