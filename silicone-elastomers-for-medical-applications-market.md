<p><p>Silicone elastomers for medical applications are classified into three main types: High-Temperature Vulcanized (HTV) silicone, Room Temperature Vulcanized (RTV) silicone, and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HTV is known for its high thermal stability and durability, making it suitable for sterilization processes. RTV offers versatility and ease of use for various applications, while LSR is favored for its precise molding capabilities, biocompatibility, and excellent mechanical properties, making it ideal for intricate medical devices and components.</p></p>
