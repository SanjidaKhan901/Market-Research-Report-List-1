<p><p>The lavender oil market can be categorized into three main types: Absolutes, Concentrates, and Blends. Absolutes are highly concentrated extracts obtained through solvent extraction, capturing the full aromatic profile of lavender. Concentrates, often referred to as 100% pure lavender oil, are derived from steam distillation, offering a potent and natural product for therapeutic use. Blends combine lavender oil with other essential oils or carriers, creating versatile formulations for aromatherapy, personal care, and culinary applications, appealing to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
