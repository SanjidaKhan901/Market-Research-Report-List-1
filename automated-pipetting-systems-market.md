<p><ul><li>Multi-Channel</li><li>Single-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated pipetting systems are categorized into two main types: multi-channel and single-channel. Multi-channel pipetting systems allow simultaneous dispensing across multiple wells, enhancing throughput and efficiency in high-throughput applications, such as drug screening and genomics. In contrast, single-channel pipetting systems offer precision and flexibility for tasks requiring meticulous handling of samples, making them ideal for applications such as PCR setup or specific reagent addition. Both systems streamline laboratory workflows, reduce human error, and improve reproducibility.</p></p>
