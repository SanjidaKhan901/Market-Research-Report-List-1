<p><p>A Network Processing Unit (NPU) is a specialized microprocessor designed to handle network data processing tasks efficiently. It offloads network-related tasks from the general-purpose CPU, allowing for faster data handling and improved overall network performance. NPUs are critical in a variety of applications, including routers, switches, and various network appliances, where their ability to process packets, manage traffic, and perform deep packet inspection is essential.</p><p>The NPU market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing data traffic, the expansion of cloud services, and the demand for high-speed networking solutions. Innovations in artificial intelligence and machine learning are further propelling the development of advanced NPUs, integrating capabilities that enhance network intelligence and automation.
