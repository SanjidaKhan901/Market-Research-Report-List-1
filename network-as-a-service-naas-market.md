<p><p>Network-as-a-Service (NaaS) is a cloud-based service model that provides network infrastructure and management capabilities over the internet. It enables businesses to access and manage networking resources without the need for physical hardware. LAN-as-a-Service (LANaaS) focuses on providing local area network infrastructure, facilitating connectivity and management within specific locations. WAN-as-a-Service (WANaaS), on the other hand, delivers wide area network capabilities, enabling communication over larger geographical areas, often optimizing traffic through various underlying technologies like SD-WAN.</p></p>
