<p><p>The High-Temperature Camera System market comprises two main types: Real Vision Camera Systems and Thermographic Vision Camera Systems. Real Vision Camera Systems provide high-resolution imagery for monitoring processes at elevated temperatures, enabling detailed visual analysis. In contrast, Thermographic Vision Camera Systems specialize in detecting infrared radiation, allowing users to measure temperature variations without direct contact. Both systems are essential in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and energy, facilitating safety, maintenance, and efficiency in high-temperature environments.</p></p>
