<p><strong>The Radiation Sterilization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-Beam Radiation Sterilization</li><li>X-Ray Sterilization</li><li>Gamma Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation sterilization equipment market comprises three main types: E-Beam radiation, X-Ray sterilization, and Gamma sterilization. E-Beam radiation utilizes high-energy electrons to sterilize medical devices quickly, ensuring minimal heat transfer. X-Ray sterilization employs high-energy X-rays, offering similar benefits with deeper penetration, ideal for complex products. Gamma sterilization, using radioactive isotopes like Cobalt-60, is widely used for its effectiveness on various materials, achieving thorough sterilization over larger volumes. Each method has distinct advantages depending on application needs.</p></p>
