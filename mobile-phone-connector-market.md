<p><p>The mobile phone connector market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions. FPC (Flexible Printed Circuit) connectors enable lightweight, space-efficient connections. Board-to-board connectors facilitate communication between circuit boards. I/O (Input/Output) connectors manage data and power transfer. Card connectors are designed for removable memory or SIM cards. Power connectors supply energy to the device, while RF (Radio Frequency) connectors support wireless communication. Together, these connectors play a crucial role in enhancing mobile device performance and functionality.</p></p>
