In contrast, Automatic Backwash Prefilters are equipped with sensors and controls that automate the cleaning cycle, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor. Both types serve to remove particulates from water systems, prolonging the life of downstream equipment and improving overall water quality.</p></p>
