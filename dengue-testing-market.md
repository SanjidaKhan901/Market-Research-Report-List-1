RT-PCR based tests focus on detecting viral RNA, providing rapid results during the acute phase of infection. Dengue IgG/IgM rapid tests are point-of-care diagnostics that deliver quick results for the presence of antibodies, facilitating immediate clinical decision-making. Each type serves distinct needs in terms of accuracy, speed, and timing in diagnosing dengue infections.</p></p>
