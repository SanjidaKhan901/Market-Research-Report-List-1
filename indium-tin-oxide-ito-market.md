<p><p>Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) is a transparent conductive material primarily used in displays, touchscreens, and solar cells. The ITO market includes several deposition techniques. Low-temperature vacuum deposition enables the application of ITO films at reduced temperatures, beneficial for heat-sensitive substrates. Sputtering technique involves bombarding a target ITO source with ions, producing high-quality films with excellent uniformity. Electron beam evaporation uses focused electron beams to vaporize ITO, allowing precise layer control and high deposition rates, making it suitable for advanced applications.</p></p>
