<p><p>Date Desensitization Technologies encompass methods that protect sensitive data by altering its representation while retaining its essential structure. Dynamic Masking involves real-time data alteration, ensuring users only access anonymized information during transactions, thereby preserving data integrity. In contrast, Static Masking creates a fixed, anonymized copy of data, allowing for the safe use of non-sensitive information in development or testing environments. Both methods are crucial for compliance and safeguarding personal data in various industries.</p></p>
