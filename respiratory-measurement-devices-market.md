<p><p>The respiratory measurement devices market includes several key types essential for monitoring respiratory health. Pulse oximeters assess blood oxygen saturation, providing quick insights into respiratory function. Spirometers measure lung function by assessing airflow and volume, crucial for diagnosing respiratory conditions. Capnographs monitor carbon dioxide levels in exhaled breath, aiding in anesthesia and critical care. Peak flow meters evaluate maximum expiratory flow, helping manage asthma. Together, these devices contribute to effective respiratory management and diagnosis across various healthcare settings.</p></p>
