Non-metallic enclosures, typically crafted from materials like fiberglass or polycarbonate, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for applications requiring insulation. Both types serve critical roles in safely housing electrical components, protecting them from environmental damage while ensuring operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
