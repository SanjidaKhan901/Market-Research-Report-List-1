<p><p>The high-strength steel sheets market is categorized into several types based on their microstructure and mechanical properties. Dual Phase (DP) steel features a mix of soft and hard phases, offering excellent formability and strength. Transformation-induced Plasticity (TRIP) steel leverages the transformation of retained austenite to enhance ductility and toughness. Complex Phase (CP) steel combines various phases to achieve a balance of strength and toughness. Other types include advanced high-strength steels with unique properties for specific applications in automotive and structural industries.</p></p>
