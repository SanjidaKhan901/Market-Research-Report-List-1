<p><p>The gluten-free bread products market is primarily categorized into two types: no-gluten bread and little-gluten bread. No-gluten bread is completely free from gluten, catering to individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, using alternative flours like rice or almond. Little-gluten bread contains minimal gluten levels, often made with wheat flour that has reduced gluten content, appealing to those with mild gluten intolerance. Both segments address the growing demand for gluten-free options while accommodating various dietary needs.</p></p>
