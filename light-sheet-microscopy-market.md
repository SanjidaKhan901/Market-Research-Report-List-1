<p><p>Light Sheet Microscopy (LSM) encompasses various types, including Single Plane Illumination Microscopy (SPIM) and Dual-View SPIM (DiSPIM). SPIM focuses on illuminating a specimen with a thin sheet of light, enabling rapid imaging of biological samples with minimal phototoxicity. DiSPIM enhances this by capturing two views simultaneously, improving spatial resolution and data quality. Other market types may include variations like selective plane illumination microscopy (SPIM) with different optical setups, catering to diverse research needs in biology and material science.</p></p>
