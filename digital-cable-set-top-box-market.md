<p><p>The Digital Cable Set-Top Box market is divided into two main types: OTT (Over the Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT refers to content delivered over the internet, enabling users to access streaming services directly without traditional cable subscriptions. DTT, on the other hand, involves broadcasting signals directly to antennas, allowing viewers to receive digital channels without needing a cable service. Both types cater to different viewing preferences, shaping the evolving landscape of digital content delivery.</p></p>
