<p><p>The TEE ultrasound probes disinfectors market is segmented into two main categories: Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) and High-Level Disinfection (HLD). LLDs/ILDs are designed for routine cleaning and disinfection of probes used on non-critical surfaces, ensuring safety and reducing infection risk. In contrast, HLD systems are employed for more stringent disinfection of probes that contact mucous membranes, effectively eliminating all microbial life. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining hygiene standards in healthcare settings.</p></p>
