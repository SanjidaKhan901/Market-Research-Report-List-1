<p><strong>The Titanium Dioxide Pigment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium dioxide pigment market primarily consists of two production processes: sulfate and chloride. The sulfate process involves treating titanium ore with sulfuric acid, resulting in a lower-quality pigment with higher impurities and environmental concerns. In contrast, the chloride process utilizes titanium feedstock and chlorine, producing a purer pigment with superior brightness and durability. As industries increasingly prioritize quality and environmental sustainability, the chloride process is gaining popularity, driving innovation and growth in the titanium dioxide pigment sector.</p></p>
