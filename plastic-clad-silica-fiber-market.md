<p><p>The Plastic-Clad Silica Fiber (PCF) market can be categorized into two main types: UV/VIS and VIS/NIR. UV/VIS fibers are designed to transmit ultraviolet and visible light, making them suitable for applications in spectroscopy and medical diagnostics. On the other hand, VIS/NIR fibers are optimized for visible to near-infrared light transmission, commonly used in telecommunications and sensor technologies. Each type addresses specific requirements in terms of light wavelength, sensitivity, and durability, catering to diverse industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
