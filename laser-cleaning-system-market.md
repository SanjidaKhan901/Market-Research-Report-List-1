<p><strong>The Laser Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Laser</li><li>Solid Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser cleaning system market is primarily categorized into gas lasers and solid lasers. Gas lasers use a gas medium, such as carbon dioxide, to generate high-energy beams effective for removing coatings, rust, or contaminants from surfaces, making them ideal for industrial applications. Solid lasers, on the other hand, utilize solid-state materials like neodymium-doped crystals, offering higher intensity and precision, suitable for delicate tasks. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and heritage conservation, enhancing efficiency and surface quality.</p></p>
