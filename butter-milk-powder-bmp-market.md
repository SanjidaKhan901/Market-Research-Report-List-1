<p><p>The Butter Milk Powder (BMP) market is categorized into three main types: Skim Buttermilk Powder, Low Fat Buttermilk Powder, and Full Cream Buttermilk Powder. Skim Buttermilk Powder is made from the residue after cream removal, offering low fat content. Low Fat Buttermilk Powder contains moderate fat levels, providing a balance between creaminess and health considerations. Full Cream Buttermilk Powder retains higher fat content, delivering a rich flavor and texture, making it ideal for gourmet cooking and baking applications.</p></p>
