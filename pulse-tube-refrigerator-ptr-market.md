<p><p>A Pulse Tube Refrigerator (PTR) uses a regenerator and a pulse tube to achieve cryogenic temperatures without mechanical moving parts, offering high reliability and efficiency. The market for PTRs can be divided into two main types: Single-Stage Pulse Tube Refrigerators, which provide cooling in a single cycle for applications like superconducting magnets, and Two-Stage Pulse Tube Refrigerators, designed for lower temperatures, catering to advanced research and medical imaging applications. Both types are essential in various industries requiring low-temperature solutions.</p></p>
