<p><p>Wood chip heating systems encompass various market types, including boilers, stoves, fireplaces, and biomass gasifiers. Boilers are designed to provide central heating and hot water by burning wood chips, making them ideal for larger spaces. Stoves offer localized heating, suitable for smaller areas. Fireplaces focus on aesthetic appeal and ambiance while providing warmth. Biomass gasifiers convert wood chips into gas for efficient energy production, promoting sustainable heating solutions. Together, these systems contribute to renewable energy and reduced carbon footprints.</p></p>
