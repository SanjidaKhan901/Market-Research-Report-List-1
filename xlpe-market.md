<p><strong>The XLPE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silane Cross-linked</li><li>Peroxide Cross-linked</li><li>Radiation Cross-linked</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) market is segmented into Silane, Peroxide, and Radiation cross-linked types. Silane cross-linked XLPE uses moisture to form bonds, offering flexibility and ease of processing. Peroxide cross-linked XLPE employs heat-activated peroxides, providing high thermal stability and mechanical strength, ideal for high-voltage cables. Radiation cross-linked XLPE uses electron beams for cross-linking, ensuring precise control and uniform properties, suitable for specialized applications. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs, balancing cost, performance, and application-specific requirements.</p></p>
