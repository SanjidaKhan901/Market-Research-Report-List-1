<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The AMOLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMOLED display market is segmented into two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diodes (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays use a matrix of active pixels controlled individually, providing superior image quality, faster refresh rates, and energy efficiency, making them ideal for smartphones and televisions. In contrast, PMOLED technology utilizes a simpler design with passive pixel control, resulting in lower resolution and brightness, primarily suited for smaller devices like wearables and simple displays.</p></p>
