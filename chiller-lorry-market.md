Emerging markets in Asia-Pacific and Latin America are also contributing to the expansion of the chiller lorry market due to increasing urbanization and changing consumer preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>The Chiller Lorry market is segmented into Single Temperature and Multi-Temperature types based on cooling capabilities. Single Temperature lorries maintain a uniform temperature throughout the cargo space, ideal for transporting goods requiring consistent cooling, such as frozen or chilled products. Multi-Temperature lorries feature separate compartments with independent temperature controls, enabling the simultaneous transport of goods at different temperature ranges, like frozen, chilled, and ambient items. Both types cater to diverse logistics needs, ensuring efficient and reliable cold chain solutions for perishable goods.</p></p>
