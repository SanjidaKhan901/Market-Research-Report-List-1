<p><strong>The Commercial Airships Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Semi Rigid</li><li>Non-rigid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial airships market is categorized into three main types: rigid, semi-rigid, and non-rigid airships. Rigid airships have a strong internal framework that maintains their shape, allowing for larger capacities and increased durability. Semi-rigid airships combine elements of both rigid and non-rigid designs, featuring a partial structure that provides stability while remaining flexible. Non-rigid airships, or blimps, rely solely on gas pressure for shape maintenance, making them lighter and more maneuverable but less spacious. Each type serves distinct applications across industries.</p></p>
