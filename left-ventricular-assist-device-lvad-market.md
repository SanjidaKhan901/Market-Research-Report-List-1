<p><p>A Left Ventricular Assist Device (LVAD) is a mechanical pump used to support heart function and blood flow in individuals with severe heart failure. It works by assisting the left ventricle, allowing it to pump blood effectively throughout the body. LVADs are often utilized as a bridge to heart transplantation or as a long-term solution for patients who are not candidates for surgery.</p><p>The LVAD market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of heart failure, advancements in technology, and rising awareness of treatment options.
