<p><p>The MLCC (Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitor) market consists of Inner Electrode Paste and Outer Electrode Paste, both essential components in capacitor manufacturing. Inner Electrode Paste is primarily used for creating the internal conductive layers of the capacitor, ensuring electrical performance and stability. Conversely, Outer Electrode Paste is applied to the capacitor's surface, providing electrical connections and mechanical protection. Together, these pastes contribute to the efficiency, reliability, and performance of MLCCs across various electronic applications and industries.</p></p>
