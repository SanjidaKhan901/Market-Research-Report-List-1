<p><strong>The Blowout Preventer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Annular BOP</li><li>Ram BOP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Blowout Preventer (BOP) market primarily consists of two types: Annular BOPs and Ram BOPs. Annular BOPs are designed to seal the space around the drill pipe or casing, providing a flexible seal crucial for well control during drilling operations. Ram BOPs, on the other hand, utilize specialized "ram" devices to close off the wellbore, effectively preventing blowouts by either shearing the pipe or sealing the well. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and efficiency in oil and gas extraction.</p></p>
