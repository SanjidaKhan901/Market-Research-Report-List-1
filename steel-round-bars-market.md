<p><p>The steel round bars market consists of three main types: hot-rolled, cold-rolled, and forged steel round bars. Hot-rolled steel round bars are produced at high temperatures, offering better workability and cost-effectiveness for structural applications. Cold-rolled steel round bars are manufactured at room temperature, providing enhanced dimensional accuracy and surface finish, making them suitable for precision components. Forged steel round bars are shaped under high pressure, resulting in superior strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty applications in various industries.</p></p>
