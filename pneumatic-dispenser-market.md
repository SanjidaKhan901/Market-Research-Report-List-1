<p><p>The pneumatic dispenser market is divided into two main types: single-component and two-component dispensers. Single-component dispensers are designed to handle one type of material, making them ideal for applications requiring consistency and precision in dispensing a single substance. In contrast, two-component dispensers mix two different materials, enabling the application of adhesives, sealants, or coatings that require a chemical reaction for full strength or curing. Both types serve critical roles in various industrial sectors, enhancing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
