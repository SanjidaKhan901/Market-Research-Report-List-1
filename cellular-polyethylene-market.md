<p><ul><li>Physically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li><li>Chemically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cellular Polyethylene market is divided into two main types: Physically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam and Chemically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam. Physically cross-linked foam is produced through thermal processes, resulting in a lightweight, flexible material suitable for insulation and cushioning applications. In contrast, chemically cross-linked foam utilizes chemical reactions to achieve enhanced durability and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for automotive and construction sectors. Both types cater to specific needs in packaging, thermal insulation, and protective applications.</p></p>
