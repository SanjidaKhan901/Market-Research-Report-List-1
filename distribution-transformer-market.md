<p><strong>The Distribution Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Liquid immersed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distribution transformer market is primarily categorized into two types: dry-type and liquid-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers use air for cooling and are often installed indoors due to their safety features and minimal environmental impact. They are commonly found in urban areas and commercial buildings. Conversely, liquid-immersed transformers are cooled by a dielectric liquid, making them suitable for outdoor applications and high-capacity needs. These are widely used in utility and industrial settings for their efficiency and durability.</p></p>
