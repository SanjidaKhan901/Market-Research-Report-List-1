<p><strong>The Waterproof Safety Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"NPN"</li><li>"PNP"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Waterproof Safety Grating market is categorized into two main types: NPN and PNP. NPN refers to negative-positive-negative configurations, where current flows when the input is grounded. This type is often used in environments where the ground is at a lower voltage. In contrast, PNP configurations feature positive-negative-positive setups, allowing current to flow when the input is activated. Both types enhance safety by providing robust, durable grating solutions for wet or hazardous conditions, ensuring user protection in various applications.</p></p>
