<p><strong>The Apnea Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Screening Devices</li><li>Actigraphy Systems</li><li>PSG Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The apnea monitors market consists of three key types: Single Channel Screening Devices, Actigraphy Systems, and PSG Devices. Single Channel Screening Devices primarily assess respiratory events through basic monitoring. Actigraphy Systems utilize motion tracking to infer sleep patterns and disturbances. PSG Devices, or Polysomnography, provide comprehensive sleep studies by monitoring multiple physiological parameters, making them the gold standard for diagnosing sleep disorders. Each device serves specific needs within the overall landscape of sleep health management and apnea detection.</p></p>
