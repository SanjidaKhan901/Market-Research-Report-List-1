<p><p>Medical liability insurance encompasses various market types, including Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance. D&O insurance protects healthcare executives and board members against claims related to their management decisions, ensuring financial security against lawsuits. In contrast, E&O insurance covers healthcare professionals for negligent acts or omissions in the services they provide, safeguarding them against malpractice claims. Both types are essential for mitigating risks and ensuring organizational stability in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
