<p><p>The transradial access products market encompasses essential devices used for minimally invasive cardiovascular procedures. Catheters are flexible tubes that facilitate access to blood vessels, while guidewires provide a pathway for catheter insertion. Sheaths act as protective coverings that allow multiple procedures through a single access point. Sheath introducers help in the initial insertion of these sheaths, ensuring smooth transitions during procedures. Together, these products enhance procedural safety, reduce complications, and improve patient recovery times.</p></p>
