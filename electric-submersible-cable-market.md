<p><strong>The Electric Submersible Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ESP Flat Power Cable</li><li>ESP Round Power Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Submersible Cable market includes two primary types: ESP Flat Power Cable and ESP Round Power Cable. ESP Flat Power Cables are designed with a flat profile, offering space efficiency and enhanced heat dissipation, making them ideal for confined wellbores. ESP Round Power Cables, with their cylindrical shape, provide robust mechanical protection and flexibility, suitable for deeper or more complex well environments. Both cables are critical for powering submersible pumps in oil and gas extraction, ensuring reliable performance under high-pressure and high-temperature conditions.</p></p>
