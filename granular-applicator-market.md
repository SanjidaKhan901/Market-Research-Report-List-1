<p><p>The granular applicator market is segmented into two main types: demountable and self-propelled applicators. Demountable applicators are designed to be attached to existing machinery, offering versatility and reduced costs for users who already have compatible equipment. In contrast, self-propelled applicators are standalone units equipped with their own engines, enabling greater mobility and efficiency in various field conditions. Both types serve critical roles in precision agriculture, enhancing the application of granular fertilizers and pesticides effectively.</p></p>
