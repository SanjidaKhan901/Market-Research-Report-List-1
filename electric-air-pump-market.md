<p><ul><li>AC Electric Pump</li><li>DC Electric Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric air pump market is divided into two main types: AC electric pumps and DC electric pumps. AC electric pumps are powered by alternating current, making them ideal for stationary applications like inflating large items at home. They typically offer higher power output and faster inflation rates. In contrast, DC electric pumps operate on direct current, making them portable and suitable for use in vehicles or outdoor settings. These pumps are often battery-powered, providing convenience for on-the-go inflation needs.</p></p>
