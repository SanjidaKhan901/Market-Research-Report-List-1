<p><p>The industrial pH sensors market comprises various types, including glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors utilize a glass membrane sensitive to hydrogen ions, making them suitable for a range of liquids. ISFET sensors, based on field-effect transistor technology, offer advantages such as miniaturization and rapid response times, often used in harsh environments. Additionally, other sensor types may include optical or electrochemical sensors, expanding application versatility across industries such as water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
