<p><strong>The High Speed AOI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2D</li><li>3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-speed Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems are categorized into 2D and 3D market types. 2D AOI primarily focuses on inspecting the surface characteristics and alignment of components on printed circuit boards (PCBs) by analyzing images captured from above. In contrast, 3D AOI provides a more comprehensive inspection by utilizing structured light or laser techniques to measure the height and volume of components, allowing for detection of defects that 2D systems may miss. Both types are crucial for ensuring product quality in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
