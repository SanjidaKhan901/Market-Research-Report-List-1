<p><strong>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs</li><li>FOG-based IMUs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) sensors market includes primarily two types: MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs and FOG-based IMUs. MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs utilize microelectromechanical systems to provide compact, cost-effective, and reliable motion sensing, making them suitable for standard automotive applications. Conversely, FOG-based IMUs use fiber optic technology for high precision and stability, ideal for advanced navigation systems. Both types play critical roles in enhancing vehicle dynamics, safety, and autonomous driving capabilities, catering to varying levels of performance and application requirements.</p></p>
