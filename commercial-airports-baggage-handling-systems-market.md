<p><strong>The Commercial Airports Baggage Handling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barcode</li><li>RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial airports utilize baggage handling systems (BHS) to efficiently manage passenger luggage from check-in to aircraft loading. The market for these systems is primarily divided into two types: Barcode and RFID (Radio Frequency Identification). Barcode systems involve scanning printed labels on baggage for tracking, providing a low-cost solution but facing limitations in speed and accuracy. In contrast, RFID systems use radio waves for real-time tracking, offering enhanced visibility, reduced handling errors, and improved operational efficiency, albeit at a higher initial investment.</p></p>
