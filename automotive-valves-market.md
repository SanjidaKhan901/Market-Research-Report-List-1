<p><p>The automotive valves market includes various types essential for vehicle performance. Solenoid valves control fluid flow electronically, enhancing precise operation. EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) valves reduce emissions by recirculating exhaust gases back to the engine. Brake combination valves improve braking efficiency by managing hydraulic pressure. AT (Automatic Transmission) control valves facilitate smooth gear transitions in automatic vehicles. Engine valves, crucial for the intake and exhaust processes, regulate airflow, impacting engine performance and efficiency. Each plays a vital role in modern vehicle functionality.</p></p>
