<p><p>The Mechanical Extract Ventilation (MEV) system market is divided into decentralized and centralized systems. Decentralized MEV systems operate independently in individual rooms, providing localized ventilation and improving air quality without extensive ductwork. In contrast, centralized MEV systems use a single unit to regulate airflow across multiple spaces, ensuring uniform ventilation throughout a building. Both types focus on energy efficiency, indoor air quality, and compliance with building regulations, catering to different needs based on space and application requirements.</p></p>
