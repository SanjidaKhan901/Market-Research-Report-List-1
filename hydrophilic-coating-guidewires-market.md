Straight guidewires are designed for linear advancement through vascular pathways, offering enhanced navigation in straight vessels. In contrast, elbow guidewires feature a curved design, facilitating maneuverability in more complex anatomical structures. Both types utilize hydrophilic coatings to reduce friction, improve lubrication, and enhance their ability to traverse tortuous pathways, making them essential in minimally invasive procedures and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
