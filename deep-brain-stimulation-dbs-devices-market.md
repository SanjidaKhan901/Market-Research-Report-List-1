<p><p>Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) devices are categorized primarily into single-channel and dual-channel systems. This dual approach can enhance therapeutic outcomes and expand treatment possibilities for patients with multifaceted symptoms or multiple conditions, thus influencing market dynamics and growth.</p></p>
