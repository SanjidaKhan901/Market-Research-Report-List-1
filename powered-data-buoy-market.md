<p><p>The Powered Data Buoy market is primarily categorized into two types: solar-powered and battery-powered. Solar-powered buoys utilize photovoltaic cells to harness sunlight, making them environmentally sustainable and ideal for remote locations with consistent sunlight. They offer continuous operation with low maintenance. Battery-powered buoys rely on stored energy, providing flexibility in deployment and functioning in varied environmental conditions. However, their operational lifespan is limited by battery life, requiring periodic maintenance and replacement, which can increase operational costs.</p></p>
