<p><ul><li>Fumed White Carbon Black</li><li>Precipitation White Carbon Black</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The white carbon black market primarily includes two types: fumed white carbon black and precipitated white carbon black. Fumed white carbon black is produced via vaporization of silicon compounds, leading to a fine, high surface area product used in applications like reinforcement and as a filler in rubber and plastics. Precipitated white carbon black, made through chemical processes involving silicates, is utilized for its thickening and absorption properties in coatings, adhesives, and personal care products. Both serve diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
