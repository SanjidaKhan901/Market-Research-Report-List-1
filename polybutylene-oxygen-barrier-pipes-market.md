<p><p>The Polybutylene Oxygen Barrier Pipes market comprises two main types: Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H is characterized by its high density and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring durability and resistance to pressure. In contrast, PB-R offers enhanced flexibility and improved temperature resistance, making it ideal for diverse installations. Both types are primarily utilized in plumbing, heating, and cooling systems due to their effective oxygen barrier properties, which prevent corrosion and extend service life.</p></p>
