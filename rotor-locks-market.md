<p><p>Rotor locks are crucial safety devices in aviation, commonly categorized into hydraulic and electric types. Hydraulic rotor locks utilize fluid pressure to secure helicopter rotors, ensuring stability during maintenance and transport. They are known for their robustness and reliability under various conditions. In contrast, electric rotor locks operate through electronic mechanisms, offering quicker engagement and disengagement. Both types emphasize safety and efficiency, catering to diverse operational needs within the aviation industry while ensuring rotor systems remain securely locked when not in use.</p></p>
