<p><ul><li>Central Dedusting System</li><li>Independent Dust Collector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dedusting systems market comprises two primary types: central dedusting systems and independent dust collectors. Central dedusting systems are integrated solutions that manage dust collection for multiple machines or processes, ensuring efficient airflow and centralized filtration. In contrast, independent dust collectors are standalone units dedicated to specific equipment or areas, providing localized dust control. Both systems enhance air quality, improve workplace safety, and comply with environmental regulations, catering to various industrial applications, including manufacturing and processing environments.</p></p>
