<p><p>The composite doors and windows market features materials such as Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) and Wood Plastic Composites (WPC). FRP combines plastic with glass fibers, offering enhanced durability, weather resistance, and low maintenance. In contrast, WPC blends wood fibers with plastic for improved strength and aesthetics, providing a natural wood appearance with enhanced longevity. Both materials cater to residential and commercial applications, appealing to consumers seeking energy efficiency, design versatility, and sustainable building solutions.</p></p>
