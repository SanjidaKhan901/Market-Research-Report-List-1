<p><p>The protective relays market consists of three main types: electromagnetic relays, static relays, and mechanical relays. Electromagnetic relays use electromagnetic principles for operation, providing reliable protection in various electrical systems. Static relays, on the other hand, utilize solid-state components, offering faster response times and greater accuracy. Mechanical relays, characterized by their physical moving parts, are less common but still used in specific applications. Each type serves a distinct role in enhancing the safety and reliability of electrical networks.</p></p>
