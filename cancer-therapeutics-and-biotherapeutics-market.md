<p><p>Cancer therapeutics encompass various treatment modalities aimed at combating cancer. Chemotherapy involves the use of cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells. Radiation therapy employs high-energy radiation to target and destroy tumors. Hormone therapy disrupts the hormonal signals that cancer cells depend on for growth. Biotherapy, or immunotherapy, harnesses the body's immune system to recognize and attack cancer cells. Each type plays a critical role in personalized treatment plans, enhancing patient outcomes and improving survival rates.</p></p>
