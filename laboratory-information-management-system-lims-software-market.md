<p><p>The Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) software market is categorized into two primary types: on-cloud and on-premise solutions. On-cloud LIMS is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced IT maintenance requirements. In contrast, on-premise LIMS is installed locally on laboratory computers, providing full control over data security and customization but requiring significant hardware investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different laboratory needs based on operational preferences and budget constraints.</p></p>
