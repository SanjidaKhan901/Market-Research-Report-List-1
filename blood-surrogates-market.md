<p><strong>The Blood Surrogates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HBOC</li><li>PFBOC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood surrogates market is primarily categorized into HBOC (Hemoglobin-Based Oxygen Carriers) and PFBOC (Perfluorocarbon-Based Oxygen Carriers). HBOCs are derived from hemoglobin and designed to mimic red blood cells in oxygen transport. They are used in cases of blood loss or surgery. PFBOCs, on the other hand, utilize perfluorocarbon compounds to dissolve and transport oxygen, providing an alternative for patients with specific needs or in situations where blood transfusions are not viable. Each type addresses unique clinical requirements.</p></p>
