<p><strong>The Liquid Flow Measuring Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Flowmeter</li><li>Differential Pressure Flowmeter</li><li>Volume Flowmeter</li><li>Open Channel Flowmeter</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid flow measuring instruments market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Velocity Flowmeters** measure fluid speed to determine flow rate, often using technologies like electromagnetic or ultrasonic sensors.</p><p>2. **Differential Pressure Flowmeters** assess flow by measuring pressure differences across a flow restriction, typically using orifice plates or vents.</p><p>3. **Volume Flowmeters** quantify the total volume of fluid passing through a point, employing methods like positive displacement.</p><p>4. **Open Channel Flowmeters** gauge fluid flow in open channels, utilizing devices like weirs or flumes. </p><p>5. **Other market** includes specialized instruments such as mass flowmeters and thermal flowmeters, catering to specific applications.</p></p>
