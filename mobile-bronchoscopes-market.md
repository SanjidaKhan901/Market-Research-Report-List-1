<p><p>The mobile bronchoscopes market consists of two primary types: rigid and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes provide a straight, tubular design used primarily for airway surgeries, enabling direct access and visualization in the bronchial passages. Flexible bronchoscopes, on the other hand, feature a bendable design, allowing for detailed examination and intervention in hard-to-reach areas of the respiratory system. Both types play crucial roles in diagnostics, interventions, and the management of respiratory conditions, catering to varying clinical needs and patient scenarios.</p></p>
