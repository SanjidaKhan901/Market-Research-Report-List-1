<p><p>The network cables market encompasses various industrial communication protocols such as AS-i, ATM, CC-LINK, Fieldbus, and CANopen. AS-i (Actuator Sensor Interface) facilitates communication between sensors and actuators in automation. ATM (Asynchronous Transfer Mode) focuses on high-speed data transfer. CC-LINK is a Japanese protocol for factory automation, enabling seamless device communication. Fieldbus is a digital communication system used in process automation, while CANopen serves automotive and embedded applications, offering robust networking for distributed systems. Each protocol caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
