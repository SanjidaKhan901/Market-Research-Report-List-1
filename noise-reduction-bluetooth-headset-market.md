<p><strong>The Noise Reduction Bluetooth Headset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Noise Reduction</li><li>Passive Noise Reduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The noise reduction Bluetooth headset market comprises two primary types: active noise reduction (ANR) and passive noise reduction (PNR). ANR utilizes built-in microphones and advanced technology to detect ambient sounds and produce counteracting sound waves, effectively minimizing unwanted noise. In contrast, PNR relies on physical design elements, such as ear cups and insulation, to block external sounds without electronic intervention. Both types cater to different user preferences, with ANR often providing a more immersive experience, while PNR offers simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
