<p><strong>The All Dielectric Self-supporting Cable (ADSS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure ADSS</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure ADSS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>All Dielectric Self-supporting Cable (ADSS) is designed for overhead applications without the need for metallic supports, making it ideal for utility and telecommunications. The market includes Central Tube Structure ADSS, featuring a central strength member surrounded by optical fibers for optimal protection and performance. In contrast, Layer Stranding Structure ADSS consists of multiple layers of optical fibers spirally arranged around a strength member, providing enhanced flexibility and resistance to environmental factors. Both types ensure reliable data transmission in challenging conditions.</p></p>
