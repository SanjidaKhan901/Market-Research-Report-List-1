<p><strong>The Fiber Bragg Grating Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"FBG Filter"</li><li>"FBG Sensor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) mirror market is segmented into two main types: FBG filters and FBG sensors. FBG filters utilize periodic refractive index structures in optical fibers to reflect specific wavelengths of light while transmitting others, making them ideal for telecommunications and spectral filtering applications. On the other hand, FBG sensors leverage the same technology to measure physical parameters such as temperature, strain, and pressure by detecting changes in the reflected wavelength, widely used in infrastructure health monitoring and industrial applications.</p></p>
