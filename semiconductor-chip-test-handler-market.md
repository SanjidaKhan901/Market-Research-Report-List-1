<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chip Test Handler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li><li>Strip Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor chip test handlers are essential equipment in the testing process of integrated circuits. Gravity handlers use gravity to position and transfer chips efficiently. Turret handlers employ rotating platforms to manage multiple test sites, increasing throughput. Pick-and-place handlers utilize robotic arms to pick chips from trays and place them on test sockets, ensuring precision. Strip handlers are designed for handling long strips of chips, allowing simultaneous testing of multiple devices, optimizing space and testing efficiency in laboratories.</p></p>
