<p><p>The thermal analysis equipment market encompasses various types of analyzers used to study material properties under temperature changes. Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) measures heat flow to determine thermal transitions. Thermogravimetric Analyzers (TGA) assess weight changes with temperature. Simultaneous Thermal Analyzers combine DSC and TGA functions. Differential Thermal Analyzers (DTA) analyze temperature differences between a sample and a reference. Thermomechanical Analyzers (TMA) evaluate dimensional changes, while Dynamic Mechanical Analyzers (DMA) assess mechanical properties under varying temperatures. Other devices include evolved gas analysis systems and specific application tools.</p></p>
