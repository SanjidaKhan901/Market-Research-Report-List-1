<p><p>The cemented carbide plate market is categorized into coarse grain tungsten carbide (WC) and fine grain tungsten carbide (WC). Coarse grain WC is characterized by larger grain sizes, offering enhanced toughness and resistance to impact, making it suitable for tools subjected to heavy wear. In contrast, fine grain WC features smaller grain sizes, which provide improved hardness and wear resistance, ideal for precision cutting applications. Both types cater to different industrial needs, influencing their usage in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
