<p><p>The controlled-release fertilizers market includes various types designed to optimize nutrient release. Sulphur-coated urea (SCU) features a layer of sulfur that slows down urea release, improving nitrogen efficiency and reducing leaching. Polymer-coated products utilize synthetic polymers to encapsulate nutrients, allowing for gradual release based on environmental conditions. Polymer/sulphur-coated products combine both technologies, enhancing nutrient availability while minimizing losses. These advancements cater to diverse agricultural needs, promoting sustainable farming practices and improving crop yields.</p></p>
