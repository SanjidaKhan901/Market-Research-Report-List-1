<p><p>The Platform Screen Doors market consists of three main types: Full-Closed Type, Semi-Closed Type, and Half-Height Type. Full-Closed Type doors provide complete separation between the platform and tracks, enhancing safety and climate control. Semi-Closed Type doors offer partial coverage, balancing accessibility and safety. Half-Height Type doors are shorter, allowing for easier passenger interaction while still offering some protection. Each type serves different transit needs, with variations in design, functionality, and installation suited to various environments and operational requirements.</p></p>
