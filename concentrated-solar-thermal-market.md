<p><p>The concentrated solar thermal market includes several key technologies. Parabolic troughs utilize curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver pipe, heating a fluid for electricity generation. Tower systems, or heliostats, employ flat mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a central tower's receiver, producing high temperatures. Other systems may include linear Fresnel reflectors and dish Stirling systems, each with unique designs and applications. Together, these technologies harness solar energy efficiently for renewable power production.</p></p>
