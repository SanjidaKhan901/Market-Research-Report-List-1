<p><strong>The Nerve Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EEG</li><li>Evoked Potential</li><li>EMG</li><li>ECoG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nerve monitoring devices market encompasses several key types: </p><p>EEG (Electroencephalography) measures electrical activity in the brain, aiding in diagnosing neurological disorders. Evoked Potential monitors the brain's response to stimuli, useful in assessing sensory and motor pathways. EMG (Electromyography) evaluates muscle activity and nerve function, helping diagnose neuromuscular disorders. ECoG (Electrocorticography) records electrical activity directly from the cortical surface, often used in epilepsy treatment. Together, these technologies enhance surgical precision, improve patient outcomes, and facilitate timely medical interventions.</p></p>
