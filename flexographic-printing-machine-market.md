<p><ul><li>Unit -type Machine</li><li>Central Impression Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexographic printing machine market comprises various types, primarily categorized into unit-type machines and central impression machines. Unit-type machines feature multiple individual printing units that allow for flexibility in changing configurations and designs, making them ideal for short to medium runs. In contrast, central impression machines utilize a central roller that applies ink across multiple stations, allowing for high-speed, high-volume production with consistent print quality. Both types cater to diverse packaging and labeling needs in the printing industry.</p></p>
