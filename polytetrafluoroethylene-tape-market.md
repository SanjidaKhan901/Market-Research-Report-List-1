<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) tape is categorized into four market types based on density: Low Density, Standard Density, Medium Density, and High Density. Low Density PTFE tape offers flexibility and is commonly used for tasks requiring easy wrapping. Standard Density balances strength and flexibility, making it versatile for various applications. Medium Density PTFE tape provides enhanced durability and resistance to chemicals, suitable for demanding environments. High Density PTFE tape features superior strength and is ideal for extreme conditions, such as high temperatures and pressures.</p></p>
