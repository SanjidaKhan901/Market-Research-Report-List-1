<p><p>The wet processing market encompasses various segments that enhance material processing through water-related methods. Wet classification involves separating materials based on particle size using water. Water and sludge management focuses on treating and recycling wastewater and managing sludge effectively. Screening utilizes water to separate unwanted materials from desired particles. Scrubbing and attrition facilitate the removal of contaminants through agitation and friction. Finally, feeding and conveying systems ensure efficient transportation of materials throughout the wet processing stages.</p></p>
