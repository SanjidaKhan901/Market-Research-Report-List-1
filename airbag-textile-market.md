<p><p>The Airbag Textile Market primarily consists of two types: Flat Airbag Fabric and OPW (Open Pore Woven) Airbag Fabric. Flat Airbag Fabric is designed for traditional airbag systems, offering excellent durability and tear resistance, essential for reliable performance during deployment. In contrast, OPW Airbag Fabric features an open pore design, enhancing breathability and reducing weight, making it suitable for advanced airbag technologies that require improved flexibility and comfort. Both fabric types play crucial roles in automotive safety applications.</p></p>
