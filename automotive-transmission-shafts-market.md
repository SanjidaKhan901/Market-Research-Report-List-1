<p><strong>The Automotive Transmission Shafts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Velocity Joint Transmission Shafts</li><li>Lardan University Joint Transmission Shafts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive transmission shafts market is primarily divided into Constant Velocity Joint (CVJ) transmission shafts and Lardan University Joint (LUJ) transmission shafts. CVJ shafts enable smooth power transfer to wheels, allowing for flexible angles and load adjustments, making them essential for front-wheel-drive vehicles. Conversely, LUJ shafts, typically used for more specialized applications, provide durability in heavy-duty contexts. Both types are crucial for optimizing vehicle performance, efficiency, and handling, serving distinct roles in various automotive designs.</p></p>
