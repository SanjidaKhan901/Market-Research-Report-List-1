<p><p>Seed testing services encompass various market types essential for ensuring seed quality and viability. The purity test assesses the seed’s composition, identifying the proportion of pure seed vs. weed and other seeds. The water test measures moisture content, crucial for storage and germination potential. The dynamic test evaluates seed performance under varying conditions. The germination test determines the percentage of seeds that sprout under standardized conditions. Other market services might include vigor testing and disease screening, contributing to overall seed health.</p></p>
