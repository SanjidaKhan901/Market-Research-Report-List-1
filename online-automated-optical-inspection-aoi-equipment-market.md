<p><strong>The Online Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Online Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: 2D AOI equipment and 3D AOI equipment. </p><p>2D AOI equipment focuses on two-dimensional imaging to inspect the surface and alignment of printed circuit boards (PCBs) and other components, ensuring quality control by detecting defects. In contrast, 3D AOI equipment utilizes advanced imaging techniques, such as structured light, to create a three-dimensional analysis of components, providing deeper insights into height and volume deviations, thus enhancing fault detection and improving overall manufacturing accuracy.</p></p>
