<p><p>Capacitive position sensors are crucial in detecting the position of objects through changes in capacitance. The market is divided into two main types: linear position sensors and angular position sensors. Linear position sensors measure the displacement along a straight path, providing precise position data for applications like industrial automation. Angular position sensors, on the other hand, detect rotational movement, making them ideal for robotics and automotive systems. Both types are essential for enhancing accuracy and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
