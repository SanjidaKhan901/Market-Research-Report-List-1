<p><p>The Explosion Protection Equipment market includes three key types: Explosion Containment, Explosion Prevention, and Explosion Segregation. Explosion Containment equipment is designed to manage and contain explosions within a defined area, minimizing damage and risks. Explosion Prevention systems prevent the conditions that can lead to an explosion, using various control measures and safety protocols. Explosion Segregation involves separating hazardous materials or processes to reduce the likelihood of an explosive incident, enhancing overall safety in industrial settings.</p></p>
