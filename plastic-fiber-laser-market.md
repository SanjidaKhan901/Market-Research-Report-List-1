<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic fiber laser market is categorized primarily into Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant beam of light, providing consistent output useful for applications requiring steady energy, such as cutting and engraving. Pulsed fiber lasers, on the other hand, generate energy in short bursts, allowing for greater control and precision in processes like marking and welding. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing manufacturing efficiency and material processing capabilities.</p></p>
