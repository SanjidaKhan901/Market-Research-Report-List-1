<p><strong>The Ducting Reducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concentric Reducer</li><li>Eccentric Reducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ducting reducer market includes two primary types: concentric and eccentric reducers. Concentric reducers maintain a central axis, seamlessly transitioning from a larger to a smaller pipe diameter, ensuring uniform flow and minimal turbulence. Eccentric reducers, on the other hand, feature a flat side that aids in draining and venting, making them ideal for applications where flow direction is critical. Both types are essential in HVAC and plumbing systems, optimizing airflow and system efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
