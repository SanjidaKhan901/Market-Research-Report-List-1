<p><p>An Auto Transformer Rectifier Unit (ATRU) is used to convert AC to DC in traction systems, particularly in railways and electrified transport. The leading market types are categorized by the number of pulse configurations: 6 Pulse, 12 Pulse, and 18 Pulse. The 6 Pulse ATRU provides a simple design with basic harmonics, while the 12 Pulse and 18 Pulse configurations utilize phase-shifting transformers to reduce harmonics, improving efficiency and performance in high-demand applications. Each type serves various operational requirements and levels of power quality.</p></p>
