<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Run-flat Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting Technology</li><li>Self-sealing Technology</li><li>Auxiliary-supported Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive run-flat tires market features three main types: </p><p>1. **Self-supporting Technology** utilizes reinforced sidewalls that maintain structural integrity after air loss, allowing continued driving for a limited distance.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Self-sealing Technology** incorporates a sealing layer that automatically plugs punctures, reducing air loss and enabling short-term use without immediate repair.</p><p>3. **Auxiliary-supported Technology** includes an additional support ring or structure to prevent tire collapse, allowing vehicles to travel even after a significant loss of pressure. Each technology enhances safety and mobility post-puncture.</p></p>
