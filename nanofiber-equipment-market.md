<p><p>The nanofiber equipment market includes three primary types: melt-blown machines, electrospinning equipment, and others. Melt-blown machines utilize a process that extrudes melted polymer to create fine fibers, ideal for filtration and medical applications. Electrospinning equipment employs an electric field to draw polymer solutions into nanofibers, offering versatility in producing high-surface-area materials for textiles and composites. Other equipment may encompass various technologies and methods for fabricating nanofibers, catering to specific industrial needs and innovations.</p></p>
