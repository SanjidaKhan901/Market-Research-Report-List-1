<p><p>The industry sterilization equipment market encompasses various methods for eliminating contaminants. Heat sterilization equipment uses high temperatures to kill microorganisms, often through autoclaving. Chemical sterilization employs gases or liquid chemicals for disinfection, suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Filtration sterilization involves removing pathogens via specialized filters, commonly used in pharmaceuticals. Ionizing radiation sterilization utilizes gamma rays or electron beams to disrupt DNA in microorganisms, ensuring sterile products. Each method serves specific applications across healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and food industries to ensure safety and compliance.</p></p>
