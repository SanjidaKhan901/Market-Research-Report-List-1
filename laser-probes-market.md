<p><ul><li>Pulsed Laser Probe</li><li>Continuous Wave Laser Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser probes market is primarily categorized into two types: pulsed laser probes and continuous wave laser probes. Pulsed laser probes emit energy in short bursts, providing high peak power suitable for applications requiring precision and minimal thermal damage, such as material surface analysis. In contrast, continuous wave laser probes deliver a constant beam of light, offering steady energy output ideal for ongoing measurements and processes, including spectroscopy and industrial cutting. Both types cater to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
