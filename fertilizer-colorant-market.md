<p><p>The fertilizer colorant market is categorized into two main types: liquid and powder. Liquid fertilizers are typically easier to apply and integrate well with irrigation systems, providing uniform color distribution and enhancing visual appeal. They are often preferred for precision farming. Powder fertilizers, on the other hand, offer longer shelf life and can be more cost-effective. They require mixing with water or other carriers before application, allowing for customized use in diverse agricultural settings. Each type serves specific agricultural needs and preferences.</p></p>
