<p><p>The railway vehicle wheels market comprises primarily two types: rolling steel wheels and cast steel wheels. Rolling steel wheels are manufactured through a forging process, providing high strength and durability, making them suitable for high-speed and heavy-load applications. In contrast, cast steel wheels are produced using casting methods, offering excellent machinability and design flexibility, typically used for lighter and medium-duty rail vehicles. Both types cater to distinct operational needs in the railway industry, ensuring efficient performance and safety.</p></p>
