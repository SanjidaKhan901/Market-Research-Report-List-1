<p><p>An above knee prosthesis, also known as a transfemoral prosthesis, is a type of artificial limb designed for individuals who have undergone amputation above the knee joint. This prosthesis replaces the leg from the knee upwards, providing support and mobility through advanced biomechanics and materials. Designed to mimic natural movement, these devices incorporate various technologies to enhance comfort and functionality, catering to both active and passive users.</p><p>The Above Knee Prosthesis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period, driven by several factors including an aging population, increasing prevalence of diabetes and vascular diseases, and advancements in prosthetic technology. Innovations such as microprocessor-controlled knees and lightweight materials are enhancing user experience and mobility, becoming increasingly popular among amputees. Additionally, the growing awareness of rehabilitation options and improved healthcare infrastructure are contributing to market expansion. Emerging markets, specifically in Asia-Pacific and Latin America, also present growth opportunities due to rising healthcare expenditures and accessibility to prosthetic solutions. Overall, the above knee prosthesis market is poised for substantial growth as it continues to evolve with technological advancements and increasing demand for personalized patient care.</p></p>
