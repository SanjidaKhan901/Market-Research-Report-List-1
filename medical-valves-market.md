<p><ul><li>One Way Valve</li><li>Multi Way Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical valves market comprises various types, primarily one-way and multi-way valves. One-way valves allow the flow of fluids in a single direction, preventing backflow, which is crucial in applications like IV systems and respiratory devices. Multi-way valves, on the other hand, facilitate the control of fluid flow in multiple directions, enabling complex fluid management in surgical and diagnostic equipment. Together, these valves ensure the safe and efficient handling of liquids and gases in healthcare settings.</p></p>
